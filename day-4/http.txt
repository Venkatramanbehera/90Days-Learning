ðŸ“Œ Status Codes: The Language of Debugging If you want to debug a system, look at the codes:
    - 4xx (Client Error): The user asked for something wrong (e.g., 404 Not Found, 403 Forbidden).
    - 5xx (Server Error): The system failed (e.g., 502 Bad Gateway, 500 Internal Error).
    - Key Insight: If your alarms are firing 500s, wake up the engineer. If they are firing 400s, check the user's input.
